Faculties

A Faculty of Dentistry is part of a university that specializes in the education and training of dentists. At the heart of its mission is imparting sound theoretical knowledge and an extensive repertoire of clinical skills so that students are enabled to diagnose, treat and prevent oral diseases. In this model, the faculty also teaches clinics, where students are included in a hands-on experience of working with patients with the support of experienced faculty, building their skills and confidence.

The Faculty of Computer Science stands as a dynamic center within the university, dedicated to cultivating the next generation of tech innovators and advancing the frontiers of computing. It's a place where students delve into the intricacies of software development, explore the vast potential of artificial intelligence and data science, and learn to safeguard digital information through cybersecurity studies.

The Engineering faculty exceptional student experience is driven by low student-to-professor ratios, strong ties to industry, a strong commitment to teaching, and exposure to current and relevant curricula. Engineering degrees can lead to a vast number of career opportunities, with graduates placed in almost every sector of the economy.

The Faculty has five departments:

  • Architectural Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering

An Art faculty is where students learn to create art, study art history, and explore different artistic styles. They experiment with various mediums and techniques, and often share their work with the community through exhibitions.

A Nursing faculty trains students to become nurses, teaching them how to care for patients in hospitals and communities. They also do research to improve nursing practices and advocate for better healthcare for everyone.
